SWOT ANALYSIS

Strengh: 
- Convenience Factor: Eliminates the need to travel to a physical studio.
- Low Technical Friction: Web app format means no app store download required.
- Quality Assurance: Human editors ensure photos pass strict government/embassy requirements.
- Online Photo Gallery and Printing user can upload photo to sell or buy photo from seller.
- Users can request a service to photo printing  and delivery.

Weakness: 
- Photography shop is oldest than modern market as digital photography with distant area service
- Dependence on User Hardware: Final image quality relies heavily on the user's smartphone camera and lighting.
- Labor Scalability: Relying on human editors creates a bottleneck during peak hours compared to pure AI apps.

Opportunity
- Market Gap: Very low competition in the end-to-end digital-to-physical photo ecosystem.
- B2B Partnerships: Partnering with corporate HR departments or universities for bulk employee/student ID photos.
- Global Reach: Digital-only downloads can be sold to international markets instantly.

Threats
- Rapid AI Evolution: AI image-generation tools could become advanced enough to create perfectly realistic ID photos without human editors.
- Regulatory Changes: Government bodies digitizing the entire application process (e.g., taking photos strictly at the passport office).
